object-fit

object-fit is used to specify how an img or video should fit its img tag.
object-fit property take one of the five values from below:

  • cover: the image keeps ist aspect ration and fill the given dimension. img maybe cropped to fit.
  • contain: the image keep its aspect ratio but fit within the given dimension.
  • fill: this is default. the img will stretch of squished to fit the img tag.
  • none: the img is not resized
  • scale-down: scaled down to smallest version of none or contain.

object-fit: cover

object-fit: cover; clipped or cropped the img to fit the given dimension.


object-fit: contain;

object-fit: contain; doesn't disrupt the img ratio. therefore, the smallest value of width or height is applied.


object-fit: fill;

object-fit: fill; doesn't cropped or clipped the img, stretch or squished the img to fit the given value.


object-fit: none;

object-fit: fill; cropped or clipped the img to retain the given dimension.


object-fit: scale-down;

object-fit: scale-down; similar to contain property. doesn't cropped or clipped the img but, retain the original ration.
